- Configurazione OLSR, policy routing, firewalling, troubleshooting
- Basata su: OpenWRT 12.09 Attitude Adjustment / 14.07 Barrier Breaker, AirOS ≤5.5.2, RouterOS v6.x, EdgeOS 1.6–1.10, pfSense 2.1.3
